CoffeeBoi
From OmegaWiki, the Tea Encyclopedia

 CoffeeBoi (Real name ████████ █████ ██████[1]) is a famous web designer and color theory expert. Believe it or not, he actually chose the colors for this page himself. Originally believed to be British, it has been confirmed that he is in fact American.[2]

Early Childhood

 CoffeBoi's earliest memory is at 2 years old, finishing the construction of the exterior of the Burj Khalifa. There was an incident during construction where he fell off but got caught by a really big bird. The bird turned out to be Big Bird, famous Sesame Street character.[3]
 As an elementary schooler, CoffeeBoi took the infamous "Young 'CoffeeBoi' PNG" photo. That same year, he ended up becoming friends with gbin, after parachuting down to him from a Model 281 Proteus, which he had set to crash into the school building below. After gbin critiqued his form, they later ended up crashing another into the abandoned right wing of the school.[4]
 His middle school memories before the eighth grade have been completely wiped, due to COVID-19 hitting a vital nerve in the memory of those years.[5] In the eighth grade, CoffeeBoi (at the time known as BritainBoi) met omegatea, conversing for the first time after several months of beating omegatea to a pulp after spanish class.[6]

High School and Drug Addiction

 Near the beginning of high school, CoffeeBoi began getting immensely addicted to the Schedule I drug known as the Cello. He had a short burst of addiction to Coffee, but after going through intense rehab sessions, he has managed to bring it down to a level of casual enjoyment.[7]
 CoffeeBoi's high school career could be described with the phrase "fell off", since he did literally nothing throught the entirety of his high school career.
 CoffeeBoi graduated High School with a 1.0 gpa.[8]

Personal Life

 CoffeeBoi researches color theory in his free time, and often applies his knowledge on web design. He enjoys spending his free time playing the Cello and playing video games such as games from the Animal Crossing series as well as the Metroid series.[9] It should be noted that in Animal Crossing: New Horizons, CoffeeBoi created an orchard in which villagers would often wander off to and die.
