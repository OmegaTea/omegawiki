Mycketransigium
From OmegaWiki, the Tea Encyclopedia

 Mycketransigium is an INCREDIBLY RARE, SPECIAL material with unknown origin. It holds the power of 1000 times its weight in uranium, with none of the typical risks of uranium.[1] In urban legend, it has been said to give you magical powers if you eat it. This is in fact false, and if you eat it you will explode. Oddly, ingestion for literally any sake other than eating does not seem to explode it.[2]
